Add Time Spans To Your News Search: Search Time Slices

This tool takes a year and query you input and generates date-restricted searches for several news search engines, including Google News, Google Books (Books, Newspapers, and Magazines are searched separately), Newspapers.com, and Chronicling America. The URLs will appear underneath the "Click Here To Generate Search URLs" button after you click it.
